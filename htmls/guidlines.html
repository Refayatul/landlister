Land Purchase Guidelines

Before buying land through our platform, please review these important terms and ethical guidelines to ensure a secure and responsible transaction.

1. Verify Legal Documents

Ensure that the land has a clear and updated title. All documents including deed, khatian, and mutation records should be authentic and verified by legal professionals.

2. Physical Site Visit & Survey

Physically visit the land location. It's highly recommended to conduct an independent survey to confirm plot boundaries, dimensions, local environment, accessibility (roads, utilities), and any potential encroachments or disputes.

3. Fair & Transparent Dealings

Conduct all negotiations transparently and ensure all terms are clearly documented. Avoid exploiting sellers and maintain honesty throughout the transaction process.

4. Ethical & Legal Compliance

Ensure your land purchase respects all environmental laws, land zoning regulations, and local community rights. Do not engage in practices that unfairly displace vulnerable communities.

5. Verify Tax & Utility Status

Make sure all land taxes (Khajna) and any applicable utility bills are paid up to date by the seller. Outstanding dues can cause legal complications for new owners.

6. Secure Transactions & Personal Data

Utilize secure payment methods and be cautious when sharing personal identification and financial information. Prefer transactions through verified agents or directly via our secure platform features if available.

7. Due Diligence & Reporting Fraud

Perform thorough due diligence. If you encounter any suspicious listings, misrepresentations, or potentially fraudulent activities, please report them to our support team immediately.
