Help Center

Find answers to common questions about using LandLister, buying or selling land, and managing your account. If you can't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact us.

General Questions

LandLister is an online platform dedicated to connecting land buyers and sellers. We aim to provide a secure, transparent, and user-friendly environment for all your land transaction needs.

You can create an account by clicking the "Register" button on our homepage or login page. You'll need to provide some basic information like your name, email, and create a password.

Browsing listings and contacting sellers is generally free. Listing a property for sale may involve a fee or subscription. Please check our "Pricing" or "Sell Your Land" section for detailed information.

For Buyers

Use the search bar on our homepage or the "Properties" page. You can filter by location, land type, size, price range, and other criteria to find suitable listings.

We strongly recommend verifying all legal documents, conducting a physical site visit, checking tax status, and understanding local zoning laws. Please refer to our "Land Purchase Guidelines" page for detailed advice.

For Sellers

Once logged in, navigate to the "Sell Your Land" or "Add Property" section. You will be guided through a form to provide details about your land, including location, size, type, price, and images.

You'll need to provide accurate details such as the property title, description, full address, land area and unit, asking price, and high-quality images. Clear and comprehensive information attracts more buyers.
